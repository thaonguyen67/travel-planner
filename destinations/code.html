Hoi An Ancient Town

location_on Quang Nam Province
star 4.9 (1.2k reviews)

info Overview

Hoi An is a city on Vietnam’s central coast known for its exceptionally well-preserved Ancient Town, cut through with canals. The former port city’s melting-pot history is reflected in its architecture.
